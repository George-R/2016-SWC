Alex Rodriguez entered the MLB in 1993, drafted 1st overall by the Seattle Mariners. In 2001 signed for a record (at the time) of $252 million over ten years with the Texas Rangers. On February 15, 2004, Rodriguez was traded to the New York Yankess.

In an interview with ESPN Rodriguez admited to using banned substance from 2001 to 2003. In 2014, a report surfaced leading to the Biogenisis scandal. As a result of this new information, Rodriguez was suspended for the entire 2014 season.
